1 Meet the Age Requirement

The minimum age requirement for pilot training must be 17 years old.

2 Academic Qualifications

You must have completed your 12th grade with Physics and Mathematics.

3 Budget for CPL Flying Training

The budget for a Commercial Pilot License (CPL) training is between 30 to 65 lakhs INR. Always plan finances properly before enrolling.

4 CPL Flight Training Duration

The average duration to complete CPL training is between 12 to 24 months. This can vary depending on the flight school and your progress.

5 DGCA Pilot Medicals

You must take DGCA Class 1 and Class 2 medical examinations and be certified medically fit. Your medical history plays a major role in your aviation career.

6 DGCA Computer Number Registration

Register and obtain a DGCA Computer Number. This unique number is required for DGCA exams and training records.

7 Getwings‚Äô DGCA Pilot Ground Classes

Join Getwings‚Äô DGCA Ground Classes to master theoretical knowledge for DGCA exams. We also offer sessions in our in-house G-1000 C172 simulator to apply concepts practically.

8 Ace the DGCA Exams

DGCA exams cover subjects like Air Navigation, Aviation Meteorology, Air Regulations, Aircraft Technical General, Aircraft Technical Specific, and more. Prepare well and score high.

9 Complete Flight Training Abroad

Depending on your plan, complete your flight training abroad while adhering to DGCA requirements. This provides hands-on experience and required flying hours.

10 License Conversion

Convert your international pilot license into an Indian DGCA-recognized license. You may need around 8 flight hours and pass two DGCA exams if not completed earlier.

11 Type-Rating Certification

Get your type-rating certification to qualify you to operate specific aircraft types. This is a major milestone towards your airline career.

12 Airline Preparation

Transitioning to an airline career requires technical expertise, hours building, certifications (IR/ME), and strong interview preparation. Stay disciplined and keep learning continuously.

13 Join Indian Airlines
